About Katakanji

Research suggests that Japanese is one of the most difficult language to learn in the world. What is so difficult about Japanese as a language? Well, apart from the complicated grammar and diction, the writing system is frustratingly difficult to learn. You need to be good at katakana, hiragana and kanji. While katakana and hiragana might be consist of 'only' about 169 characters, kanji on the other hand, could reach more than 2500 characters. If you can effectively memorize 5 kanji/day, it would take you TWO years to memorize all important kanji just to be able to read newspaper. Can you imagine the struggle? There are too much to memorize. Besides, one kanji character might have a few meanings and as a learner, you need to know when and how to use kanji properly.

Japanese language experts recommended to learn kanji by finding their individual meanings and figure out how to put them into context that is acceptable by Japanese standard. Meanwhile, education experts suggests that learning has to be personalized in order to create better improvement to the learners. Everybody has to start somewhere and they start from a different point. Its also suggested that people tend to develop affinity to find information upon topics they are interested in the most.

Those consideration has lead us to the development of KataKanji, an iOS app that that helps Japanese language learners understand kanji and its context through reading articles that interest them, so that they would have a tool to help them memorize new kanji and recognize its context of use in from topics that matches their preferences!

There are three main features KataKanji uses to make your kanji learning more effective. First, we provide reading materials that matches the topics you are interested in. This way you would feel learning kanji a lot more interesting and personalized, because everyone would start and grow from their own favorite spots!

The second feature is the ability to store new kanji information that you learned throughout the use of this app, this allows you to be able to reflect back on your own milestones and progress. And the third feature available is a set of Quiz to test how well you learn kanji from one article, besides your reading comprehension would be put on test, too.

These features would ensure that you would maximize your kanji learning experience with the help of this app.
